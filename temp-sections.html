Gather Around the Table

The heart of the farmhouse beats strongest in our sun-drenched dining room, where handcrafted furniture meets panoramic views of the Rum River valley. Here, morning coffee becomes a meditation and evening meals transform into lasting memories.

With seating for eight and a rustic farm table that's hosted countless celebrations, this space invites connection. The original wood beams overhead tell stories of generations past, while modern amenities ensure every comfort for today's gatherings.

Rest in Rural Luxury

Each bedroom is a sanctuary designed for deep sleep and peaceful mornings. Wake to birdsong filtering through vintage lace curtains, with views that stretch across wildflower meadows to the forest beyond.

Premium linens, memory foam mattresses, and carefully curated antiques create an atmosphere of refined comfort. Whether you're here for adventure or escape, these rooms offer the perfect retreat after days spent exploring the untamed beauty that surrounds you.
